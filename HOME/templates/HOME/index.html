Bouncing Balls

Here is my first finished project that I started in 2020 during the panemic. I was taking a computer science class at the time and wanted to explore the subject and tried to make a simple physics engine with balls bouncing like a windows DVD logo. Admittedly this took a lot longer to implement than I hoped and I kept pushing updates for the project until I finally got it to a place I liked in 2024. Of course it could be improved, but I feel really proud of accomplishing this as my first project.

Prime Spiral

This is my second project that I've finished which is the prime spiral. Starting with 1 at the origin, it marks prime numbers green and non-prime numbers black, slowly spiralling out to the edges of the screen where it stops. Also known as the ulam spiral, it shows the suprising patterns of prime numbers emerging from the tapestry and shows visually that prime numbers are not as random as they seem.
