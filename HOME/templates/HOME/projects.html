Bouncing Balls

Here is my first finished project that I started in 2020 during the pandemic. I was taking a computer science class at the time and wanted to explore the subject and tried to make a simple physics engine with balls bouncing like a windows DVD logo. Admittedly this took a lot longer to implement than I hoped and I kept pushing updates for the project until I finally got it to a place I liked in 2024. Of course it could be improved, but I feel really proud of accomplishing this as my first project.

Prime Spiral

This is my second project that I've finished which is the prime spiral. Starting with 1 at the origin, it marks prime numbers green and non-prime numbers black, slowly spiralling out to the edges of the screen where it stops. Also known as the ulam spiral, it shows the suprising patterns of prime numbers emerging from the tapestry and shows visually that prime numbers are not as random as they seem.

SkillTree

Sword and Glory is a small mobile game that I found myself incredibly attracted to and found suprising depth of storytelling. This game has a typical leveling system where you are given a choice to choose a skill each from 3 different catagories at the start of a run and gain a new skill every level. Despite that, I thought that there must be an optimal way for gaining skills to maximize my progression. So this project came to be. I asked some friends for help with the problem and it somehow became one of the questions for my school's competitive programming competition. This project still remains as one of my favorites and helped me know so many great people. The project was then constructed into a form with a dynamic scoring system that predicts the best build depending on your playstyle and preferences.
